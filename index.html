Why GuruVex?
It started in 2004. I was looking for a avatar name I could use
on game servers, not just a server. But most, if not all servers.
I tried quite a few. After a while I came up with guruvex. After tossing
it in to google and yahoo and coming back with zero search results.
I knew I found something unique, easy, and memorable.
I started using it, and everyone started knowing me as guruvex across
many games. A lot of friends were jealous that I had such a cool name,
and it was the same everywhere I went.
After 10 years with this avatar name. it become part of me,
a online nick name.
When I started programming it just felt natural to keep using my
online name. Because its unique I was able to get it on GitHub and
anywhere else, so of course guruvex.com was just the next step.
